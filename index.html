Essential Road Safety Tips

Knowledge is your best protection on the road. Follow these guidelines to stay safe.

Defensive Driving

Always anticipate other drivers' actions. Maintain safe following distances and be prepared to react to unexpected situations.

Avoid Distractions

Put your phone away while driving. Texting while driving makes you 23 times more likely to crash.

Speed Management

Observe speed limits. For every 1km/h increase in speed, the risk of a crash increases by 3%.

Weather Awareness

Reduce speed by 1/3 on wet roads and by 1/2 on snowy roads. Increase following distances in poor conditions.
